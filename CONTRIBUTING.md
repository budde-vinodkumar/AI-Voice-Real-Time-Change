# Contributing to AI-Voice-Real-Time-Change

Thank you for your interest in contributing! To help us maintain a high-quality project, please follow these guidelines.

## How to Contribute

1. **Fork the repository** and clone your fork.
2. **Create a new branch** for your feature or fix.
3. **Make your changes** with clear commit messages.
4. **Test** your changes if applicable.
5. **Submit a Pull Request** with a description of your changes.

## Code of Conduct

By contributing, you agree to follow our [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) (if available).

## Reporting Issues

If you find a bug or have a feature request, please [open an issue](https://github.com/budde-vinodkumar/AI-Voice-Real-Time-Change/issues) with detailed information.

Thank you!
